MS Dhoni - The Cricket Legend

Introduction

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, popularly known as MS Dhoni, is one of the greatest cricketers of all time. He captained the Indian national team and led them to numerous victories.

Early Life

Born on July 7, 1981, in Ranchi, Jharkhand, Dhoni's journey from a small-town boy to a global cricket icon is inspiring. He was passionate about cricket from a young age and made his debut for the Indian team in 2004.

Achievements

  • Led India to victory in the 2007 ICC T20 World Cup.
  • Won the ICC Cricket World Cup in 2011.
  • Won the ICC Champions Trophy in 2013.

Retirement

MS Dhoni retired from international cricket on August 15, 2020, but continues to play in the Indian Premier League (IPL) for Chennai Super Kings.
